Inter-library Loan Service

The Central Library maintains excellent exchange relations with a number of libraries in the country including those in Mumbai and can borrow / procure for its users the books and periodicals not available with us. The library can also procure soft/hard copies of articles needed by our users from publishers, vendors, and/or other service providers including NISCAIR, New Delhi, British Library etc quite promptly.

In case users desire to visit any library in Mumbai, they can collect a letter of introduction from the library for this purpose.

Under collaborative arrangement, every Wednesday transport is available to visit BARC library. However, visitors need to register before hand for BARC security entry.
