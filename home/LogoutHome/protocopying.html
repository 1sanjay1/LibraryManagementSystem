  • The Central Library offers photocopying service to all its members, walk-in users and others.
  • Only single copy of journal articles and book chapters are provided for private study and research purpose.
  • The service is free to all the faculty memebers of IITB. Please contact : Incharge, Bound Vols Section for details.
  • Photo Copy Charges are as follows
    • IITB Staff and students : Re. 0.50 per page A4 size Re. 1.00 per page A3 size
    • Students from other : Re 1.00 per page A4 size
    • Colleges and Institutes : Re. 2.00 per page A3 size
    • Visitors from Corporates :Re. 2.00 per page A4 size
    • and Industries etc. Re. 4.00 per page A3 size
  • The Photocopying Unit is on the mezanine floor and its timings are

    9.30 am : 1.00 pm and 2.00 pm : 5.30 pm from Monday to Friday

  • Central Library also supplies photocopies to Institutes / individuals by post on payment.
  • Central Library also operates DEPOSIT ACCOUNT facility for regular users to obtain copies quickly. For more details, please contact: Incharge, Periodical Section.
